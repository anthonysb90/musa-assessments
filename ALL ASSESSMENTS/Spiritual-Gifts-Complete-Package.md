## 2. Scoring model

- **Each gift = sum of its 5 items**, range 0 to 15.
- **Results are ranked greatest to least.** The top gifts are simply the highest-scoring. There is no fixed score threshold, the old "13+" note was only a limitation of Jotform's inability to rank, and has been removed entirely.
- **Report always shows a top tier by rank** (top 3 featured), plus all 25 ranked below.
- **Tie-break:** equal scores break alphabetically by gift name, so ordering is stable.
- **Self-report caveat** shown on results: a low score doesn't mean the person lacks that gift, and a high score is directional, not a verdict.

## 5. Gift definitions (short) + Scripture references

- **A. Prophecy** — receive and communicate an immediate message of God to His people through a divinely anointed utterance. *Luke 7:26 · Acts 15:32 · Acts 21:9-11 · Romans 12:6 · 1 Cor 12:10, 28 · Eph 4:11-14*
- **B. Pastor** — assume long-term personal responsibility for the spiritual welfare of a group of believers. *John 10:1-18 · Eph 4:11-14 · 1 Tim 3:1-7 · 1 Pet 5:1-3*
- **C. Teaching** — communicate information relevant to the health and ministry of the Body so others learn. *Acts 18:24-28 · Acts 20:20-21 · Rom 12:7 · 1 Cor 12:28 · Eph 4:11-14*
- **D. Wisdom** — know the mind of the Spirit to apply knowledge to specific needs in the Body. *Acts 6:3,10 · 1 Cor 2:1-13 · 1 Cor 12:8 · James 1:5-6 · 2 Pet 3:15-16*
- **E. Knowledge** — discover, accumulate, analyze, and clarify information pertinent to the Body's well-being. *Acts 5:1-11 · 1 Cor 2:14 · 1 Cor 12:8 · 2 Cor 11:6 · Col 2:2-3*
- **F. Exhortation** — minister comfort, encouragement, and counsel so others feel helped and healed. *Acts 14:22 · Rom 12:8 · 1 Tim 4:13 · Heb 10:25*
- **G. Discerning of Spirits** — know whether behavior purported to be of God is divine, human, or Satanic. *Matt 16:21-23 · Acts 5:1-11 · Acts 16:16-18 · 1 Cor 12:10 · 1 John 4:1-6*
- **H. Giving** — contribute material resources to the Lord's work with liberality and cheerfulness. *Mark 12:41-44 · Rom 12:8 · 2 Cor 8:1-7 · 2 Cor 9:2-8*
- **I. Helps** — invest your talents in others' ministry so they increase their own effectiveness. *Mark 15:40-41 · Luke 8:2-3 · Acts 9:36 · Rom 16:1-2 · 1 Cor 12:28*
- **J. Mercy** — feel genuine empathy for the suffering and translate it into cheerful deeds that alleviate suffering. *Matt 20:29-34 · Matt 25:34-40 · Mark 9:41 · Luke 10:33-35 · Acts 16:33-34 · Rom 12:8*
- **K. Missionary** — minister your other gifts in a second culture. *Acts 8:4 · Acts 13:2-3 · Acts 22:21 · Rom 10:15 · 1 Cor 9:19-23*
- **L. Evangelist** — share the gospel so unbelievers become disciples and responsible members of the Body. *Acts 8:5-6 · Acts 8:26-40 · Acts 14:21 · Acts 21:8 · Eph 4:11-14 · 2 Tim 4:5*
- **M. Hospitality** — provide an open house and warm welcome to those needing food and lodging. *Acts 16:14-15 · Rom 12:9-13 · Rom 16:23 · Heb 13:1-2 · 1 Pet 4:9*
- **N. Faith** — discern with extraordinary confidence the will and purposes of God for His work. *Acts 11:22-24 · Acts 27:21-25 · Rom 4:18-21 · 1 Cor 12:9 · Heb 11*
- **O. Leadership** — set goals per God's purpose and move people to work together toward them. *Luke 9:51 · Acts 7:10 · Acts 15:7-11 · Rom 12:8 · 1 Tim 5:17 · Heb 13:17*
- **P. Administration** — understand a unit's goals and devise and execute effective plans to reach them. *Luke 14:28-30 · Acts 6:1-7 · Acts 27:11 · 1 Cor 12:28 · Titus 1:5*
- **Q. Miracles** — serve as a human intermediary through whom God performs powerful acts. *Acts 9:36-42 · Acts 19:11-20 · Acts 20:7-12 · Rom 15:18-19 · 1 Cor 12:10,28 · 2 Cor 12:12*
- **R. Healing** — serve as an intermediary through whom God cures illness apart from natural means. *Acts 3:1-10 · Acts 5:12-16 · Acts 9:32-35 · Acts 28:7-10 · 1 Cor 12:9,28*
- **S. Tongues** — speak to God in an unlearned language and/or deliver a message in one. *Mark 16:17 · Acts 2:1-13 · Acts 10:44-46 · Acts 19:1-7 · 1 Cor 12:10,28 · 1 Cor 14:13-19*
- **T. Interpretation** — make known in the vernacular the message of one who speaks in tongues. *1 Cor 12:10,30 · 1 Cor 14:13 · 1 Cor 14:26-28*
- **U. Voluntary Poverty** — renounce comfort and adopt a poverty-level lifestyle to serve God more effectively. *Acts 2:44-45 · Acts 4:34-37 · 1 Cor 13:1-3 · 2 Cor 6:10 · 2 Cor 8:9*
- **V. Singleness** — remain single and content, without undue sexual temptation. *Matt 19:10-12 · 1 Cor 7:7-8*
- **W. Intercession** — pray for extended periods regularly and see frequent, specific answers. *Luke 22:41-44 · Acts 12:12 · Col 1:9-12 · Col 4:12-13 · 1 Tim 2:1-2 · James 5:14-16*
- **X. Exorcism** — cast out demons and evil spirits. *Matt 12:22-32 · Luke 10:12-20 · Acts 8:5-8 · Acts 16:16-18*
- **Y. Service** — identify unmet needs in a task and use available resources to meet them. *Acts 6:1-7 · Rom 12:7 · Gal 6:2,10 · 2 Tim 1:16-18 · Titus 3:14*

Each gift also has a longer "Deeper Study" write-up (definition, likely roles in the church, and how to develop the gift) recovered from the original and stored in the report component's gift data. The report shows the short definition by default and the deeper study on tap-to-expand.
