## 2. Landing page (complete copy)

**Hero**
Where is your church actually headed?
Not where you hope it's headed. Where the evidence says it's headed. Church Growth gives you an honest read on your church's current stage, from decline to multiplication, based on what's really happening, not what you wish were happening.
Button: Start the Assessment

**About this assessment**
This is not a test you can pass or fail. It's a diagnostic. You'll answer 50 short statements about your church, mixed together so you can't tell which stage a question is measuring while you're answering it. That's intentional. It keeps your answers honest instead of aimed at a result.

**How it works**
1. Answer all 50 statements, about 12 to 15 minutes.
2. Each of the 5 stages gets scored independently from its own 10 questions.
3. Your highest-scoring stage is where your church actually is right now.

**What you'll learn**
Which of the 5 stages your church is in, from A Church in Decline to A Church Multiplying, and what that stage is asking of your leadership right now.

**Why it matters**
"Go therefore and make disciples of all nations" (Matthew 28:19). Every church is somewhere on the path between decline and multiplication. Knowing exactly where you are is what makes it possible to move.

## 5. Scoring model (matrix, do not alter the key)

Each question belongs to exactly one level. Scored independently, 10 questions per level, 30 points possible per level.

| Level | Questions | Max |
|---|---|---|
| Level 1 — Decline | 2, 6, 11, 15, 20, 24, 28, 34, 40, 41 | 30 |
| Level 2 — Plateaued | 4, 8, 17, 22, 26, 31, 37, 44, 46, 50 | 30 |
| Level 3 — Adding | 1, 7, 12, 16, 21, 27, 32, 35, 38, 42 | 30 |
| Level 4 — Reproducing Campuses | 5, 10, 14, 19, 23, 29, 33, 39, 45, 47 | 30 |
| Level 5 — Multiplying | 3, 9, 13, 18, 25, 30, 36, 43, 48, 49 | 30 |

- **The church's level is the column with the highest total.**
- **Close scores (within 3 to 4 points) between two adjacent levels indicate transition.** Flag this explicitly in the results rather than forcing a single label. A church transitioning between stages is a healthy, normal thing to name.
- No item is reverse-scored. Every item scores directly into its level column as written.

## 6. Results page

1. **Your Level** — the winning level, named and described, with its message (Please Stay / Please Come / Please GO).
2. **All 5 scores shown side by side** as a bar chart, so leadership can see how close the runner-up level is, not just the winner.
3. **Transition flag**, shown only when two adjacent levels are within 3 to 4 points of each other: "Your church may be in transition between [Level X] and [Level Y]. That's worth discussing with your leadership team, not a sign something's wrong."
4. **What this level means** — the full description and message for the winning level (Section 7 has the complete text for all 5).
5. **Where to focus next** — 2 to 3 practical next steps, drawn from the lowest-scoring questions within the winning level and the next level up, so it points toward the level after this one, not just describes the current one.
6. **Download the Church Growth Report** — PDF button.
7. **Give toward Mission USA church planting resources** — the platform-wide donation module (Section 10), tied naturally to Complete the Commission '33.

## 7. Level descriptions (complete text)

**Level 1: A Church in Decline**
Your church is losing ground. This is a critical season requiring honest evaluation, prayer, and a clear renewal plan. The message is: **Please Stay.** Stable, committed leadership is the most important thing right now.

**Level 2: A Church Plateaued**
Your church is holding steady but not growing. Plateau left unaddressed becomes decline. The message is: **Please Stay.** A renewed vision and intentional growth strategy are the next step.

**Level 3: A Church Adding**
Your church is growing and gaining momentum. The focus now is strengthening discipleship and building the leadership pipeline that sustains growth. The message is: **Please Come.** There is room and readiness for more.

**Level 4: A Church Reproducing Campuses**
Your church is expanding beyond its walls. You are thinking and operating at a regional level. The message is: **Please Come.** Your model is working, and expansion is the natural next step.

**Level 5: A Church Multiplying**
Your church is sending, planting, and producing other churches. This is the exponential level. The message is: **Please GO.** Your calling is to release leaders and resources into new works that will do the same.

*This assessment is a starting point for honest conversation, not a final verdict. Use your results to identify strengths, name gaps, and build a focused plan forward.*

## 10. Data and governance

- **Three tiers**, same pattern as the rest of the hub:
  - *Tier 1 (individual):* per-item responses, per-level totals, completion timestamp, optional intake fields.
  - *Tier 2:* not applicable, single-respondent instrument.
  - *Tier 3 (aggregate, anonymized, consent-gated):* distribution of CHC churches across the 5 levels, segment patterns by district or church size, movement over time if retaken. Directly feeds Complete the Commission '33 planning, since it shows how many churches are actually positioned to plant versus how many need to stabilize first.
- **Never a credentialing gate or a district ranking tool used punitively.** A church scoring Level 1 is a call to support, not a black mark on a pastor's file.
