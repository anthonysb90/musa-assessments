# Brief — Big Five

1. **Positioning.** The most-researched personality map: 5 traits + 6 facets, with a full per-trait report. Read once, returned to.
2. **Archetype.** Chart-led diagnostic.
3. **Signature visual.** The **banded 0–100 trait spectrum** — five bars over Low/Mod/High zones with a marker and low/high anchor words. The report OWNS the banded-spectrum. Its former radar is demoted/removed (D5) so Spiritual Growth keeps the radar.
4. **Cover.** Five horizontal steel bands of varying fill on a faint dot-grid; the tallest lit steel.
5. **Rhythm.** Spectrum hero → per-trait deep sections (snapshot, strengths, watch-outs, application, growth, Scripture) → 6-facet strip → signature strengths / low-preference. Long but paced with airy openers.
6. **Accent / texture.** Steel `#5A6A78` / faint dot-grid.
7. **Bespoke.** Facet domain-clusters; Scripture per trait; growth steps. (No character card, no gap.)
8. **Target pages.** Long — 9–12 pages.
9. **Must NOT look like.** Kingdom Design (both broad personality). Kept apart: Big Five = *continuous banded spectra*, steel, research-toned; KDP = *categorical type + 16-grid + emblems*, navy/gold, warmer.
