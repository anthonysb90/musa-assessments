# Brief — Church Health (team)

1. **Positioning.** A leadership team's aggregated, honest read of the church across 8 areas — including where the team agrees and where it splits. Worked through in a room, together.
2. **Archetype.** Chart-led diagnostic (aggregate).
3. **Signature visual.** The **team heat matrix** — 8 areas × N raters as a `SEQ_TEAL` heat grid, plus an **agreement-spread strip** per area (tight = aligned, wide = divided). Only this report aggregates raters.
4. **Cover.** A teal-deep heat-grid field, a few cells lit, forming an abstract building.
5. **Rhythm.** Matrix hero → per-area aggregate with spread → most-aligned and most-divided areas → team next steps. Medium.
6. **Accent / texture.** Teal-deep `#1F5E68` / grid-cell field.
7. **Bespoke (Church Health ONLY):** team aggregate; agreement/spread visualization. (No individual narrative, no character, no prayer.)
8. **Target pages.** Medium — 6–8 pages.
9. **Must NOT look like.** Spiritual Growth (both teal-deep, both diagnostic). Kept apart: Church Health = *heat matrix + spread* (aggregate, grid); Spiritual Growth = *radar Wheel* (individual, radial).
