# Brief — Church Planter (candidate, + spouse, + assessor)

1. **Positioning.** A candidate's readiness to plant across 13 traits, with a hidden validity layer for the assessor. Worked through with Mission USA.
2. **Archetype.** Composite multi-part.
3. **Signature visual.** The **readiness dial** — the weighted composite on a true gauge arc with the **tier verdict** (Ready / Develop / Elsewhere) marked, above a **primary-5 trait cluster** (weighted 2×, visually separated from the 8 secondaries). A **separate assessor-only validity panel** (candor / attention / infrequency flags) that never prints in the candidate copy.
4. **Cover.** A blueprint-fold field in clay, the dial embossed; "Candidate" vs "Assessor" edition marked.
5. **Rhythm.** Composite, sober. Dial → primary cluster → secondary traits → (assessor edition only) validity panel → prayer/Scripture.
6. **Accent / texture.** Clay `#B4703A` / blueprint fold lines.
7. **Bespoke (Planter ONLY):** validity/candor panel; readiness tier; spouse-input comparison (spouse edition); prayer. Two editions: candidate (no validity) and assessor (with validity) — the split is a hard privacy rule.
8. **Target pages.** Long — 8–12 pages.
9. **Must NOT look like.** Pastor Profile (both composite ministry diagnostics). Kept apart: Planter = *readiness dial + validity panel*, clay, blueprint; Pastor = *three-pillar architecture*, deep-navy, stone.
