# Brief — The Forgiveness Profile (EFMI, licensed)

1. **Positioning.** What is actually moving this person's heart toward forgiving a specific someone. Deeply personal, private; clinical-adjacent restraint.
2. **Archetype.** Chart-led diagnostic (restrained).
3. **Signature visual.** The **10-motivation ridgeline** — motivations ranked as a horizontal lollipop/ridge with the total on a real **30–180 gauge**, plus the **definition-comprehension verdict** as a small labeled band (accurate / near / misconception). Calm, not celebratory.
4. **Cover.** A single quiet horizon line easing upward in muted teal; no ornament. The restraint is the design.
5. **Rhythm.** Gentle, unhurried. Total gauge → ridgeline → verdict → private reflection recap (the recalled offense, handled with care). Airy.
6. **Accent / texture.** Muted teal `#4E8C93` / **no texture** (restraint).
7. **Bespoke.** Definition-comprehension verdict band; licensed-instrument attribution (verbatim, never restyled); private reflection recap; total gauge. (No prayer card, no character.)
8. **Target pages.** Medium — 5–7 pages.
9. **Must NOT look like.** Any celebratory personality report. Kept apart by restraint: no texture, muted accent, no devotional flourish, EFMI attribution preserved exactly.
