# Brief — Church Growth

1. **Positioning.** Which of 5 growth stages a church is actually in (decline → multiplication), with a transition flag. Read by a board, acted on.
2. **Archetype.** Chart-led diagnostic (stage).
3. **Signature visual.** The **five-stage ladder** — the five stages as ascending steps, the church's current stage lit, and a **transition marker** when it sits between two. Ordinal and directional; no other report is a ladder of ordered stages.
4. **Cover.** Five ascending navy steps, the current one lit gold, an arrow of travel.
5. **Rhythm.** Ladder hero → current-stage meaning → the five stages side by side → next-stage focus. Short-medium, decisive.
6. **Accent / texture.** Navy `#1B3A57` / ascending-step motif.
7. **Bespoke.** Stage model + transition flag; Scripture; next-stage action. (No individual traits, no character.)
8. **Target pages.** Short-medium — 4–6 pages.
9. **Must NOT look like.** Clinical severity ladder (both "ladders"). Kept apart hard: Church Growth is *aspirational/ascending* (navy, gold-lit, directional); Clinical is *a neutral severity scale* (slate, no ornament, support-first). One climbs; the other measures.
