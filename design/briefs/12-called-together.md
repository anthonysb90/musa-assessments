# Brief — Called Together

1. **Positioning.** How a ministry marriage is really holding up — taken privately by each spouse, shown side by side. Worked through together, slowly, sometimes with a safety concern present.
2. **Archetype.** Comparative & relational.
3. **Signature visual.** The **his/hers paired dumbbell** — each shared domain a single row with two dots (each spouse) joined by a bar; the gap is the story; the "lower of the two" convention marked. Two-column relational structure throughout.
4. **Cover.** Two interlocking rings in rose, one line each spouse, meeting at center.
5. **Rhythm.** Paired throughout — every spread has a two-column or dumbbell structure. Gentle. Shared strengths, then attention areas, then a next step for the couple.
6. **Accent / texture.** Rose `#A65A6B` / interlocking-rings wash.
7. **Bespoke.** Spouse side-by-side; the **domestic-safety notice** (surfaced early and clearly if triggered, never buried); couple next-step. (No character, no clusters.)
8. **Target pages.** Medium — 6–8 pages.
9. **Must NOT look like.** Leadership Health (both dumbbell). Kept apart: Called Together compares *two spouses* (rose, interlocking rings, relational-warm); Leadership Health compares *self vs observers* (gold, grid, diagnostic-cool). Different labels, orientation, and tone.
