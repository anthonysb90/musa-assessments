# Brief — Wired to Lead (DISC)

1. **Positioning.** How a leader is wired (DISC blend), paired to the biblical leader who led the same way. Read once, discussed with a team.
2. **Archetype.** Comparative & relational.
3. **Signature visual.** The **D/I/S/C quadrant map** — a two-axis plot (task↔people, direct↔reflective) with the person's blend plotted as a point + halo, the four dimension scores as satellite mini-bars, beside a **biblical-leader pairing card**. No other report plots on a 2×2.
4. **Cover.** The quadrant as a large plum compass-rose motif, the blend point lit; navy field.
5. **Rhythm.** Medium density. Quadrant hero → blend narrative → biblical-leader study → strengths/watch-outs → one growth challenge.
6. **Accent / texture.** Plum `#6B4E7A` / soft grid wash.
7. **Bespoke.** Biblical-character pairing card; Scripture blocks. (No gap, no clusters.)
8. **Target pages.** Medium — 5–7 pages.
9. **Must NOT look like.** Kingdom Design (also type + biblical figure). Kept apart: DISC is a *2×2 quadrant plot* in plum; KDP is *four clarity spectra + a 16-grid* in navy/gold. DISC is one page's verdict; KDP is a bound composite.
