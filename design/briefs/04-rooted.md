# Brief — Rooted

1. **Positioning.** A spiritual-maturity mirror across 8 biblical markers — how deep the roots really go. Worked through slowly, often devotionally.
2. **Archetype.** Formational & scriptural.
3. **Signature visual.** A **vertical root-depth cross-section** — a soil profile with strata lines; each of the 8 markers drawn as a root descending to a depth set by its band (Deeply Rooted → deep; Needs Watering → shallow). Literal, ownable, no other report is vertical or earth-themed.
4. **Cover.** A single deep taproot in gold line over sage-toned soil strata; horizon of light at top.
5. **Rhythm.** Slow and airy — a devotional pace. The cross-section gets a full page; then two strongest markers affirmed, two growth markers each with Scripture + a next step.
6. **Accent / texture.** Sage `#3E7C63` / earth-strata line pattern.
7. **Bespoke.** Scripture + growth-step per marker; domain-cluster grouping of the 8. (No character card, no gap.) Radar is explicitly NOT used (see D5) so it can't be confused with Spiritual Growth.
8. **Target pages.** Medium — 6–8 pages.
9. **Must NOT look like.** Spiritual Growth (both maturity/formational, both 6–8 domains). Kept apart hard: Rooted = *vertical soil cross-section*, sage, earthy; Spiritual Growth = *hexagonal Wheel*, teal-deep, radial.
