# Brief — Spiritual Growth

1. **Positioning.** The state of one's walk across 6 discipleship disciplines, drawn as a wheel. Returned to across a season.
2. **Archetype.** Chart-led diagnostic.
3. **Signature visual.** The **hexagonal Discipleship Wheel** — the report OWNS the radar. Six labeled spokes, graded rings, the walk shaded toward the rim. This is the single most important moment; give it room.
4. **Cover.** The empty wheel armature in teal-deep line on mist, one spoke lit — an invitation to fill it in.
5. **Rhythm.** The Wheel is the hero (full page). Then a per-discipline band strip, strongest two, growth two with Scripture + step. Medium density.
6. **Accent / texture.** Teal-deep `#1F5E68` / concentric ring guides.
7. **Bespoke.** Scripture + step per discipline; domain-cluster (the 6). (No character card, no gap.)
8. **Target pages.** Medium — 6–8 pages.
9. **Must NOT look like.** Rooted and Big Five (radar family). Rooted is separated by going vertical/soil; Big Five's radar is demoted (D5) so ONLY this report uses a radar as its signature.
