# Brief — Clinical Wellness Check (restrained variant)

1. **Positioning.** A brief, quarantined emotional-wellbeing screening — owner/care-only. NOT a diagnosis. Handled with clinical restraint and care.
2. **Archetype.** Restrained clinical (its own minimal variant, deliberately outside the expressive family).
3. **Signature visual.** The **severity-band ladder** — the score placed on a plainly labeled severity scale (e.g., minimal / mild / moderate / elevated), no ornament, no color celebration. If elevated, **support guidance appears first, before the score.**
4. **Cover.** No cover flourish. A plain titled first page with the instrument name and what it does/doesn't measure.
5. **Rhythm.** Minimal, calm, generous whitespace. Support-first when elevated. Attribution preserved exactly.
6. **Accent / texture.** **Slate `#5A6A78` neutral only; no texture, no icons around scores.**
7. **Bespoke.** Clinical severity band; licensed attribution (verbatim); early support/next-step guidance. (Nothing else.)
8. **Target pages.** Short — 1–3 pages.
9. **Must NOT look like.** Every other report. It is intentionally the plainest thing in the family. Phase 4 governs it. See decisions-log for any place a score risked reading as a diagnosis.
