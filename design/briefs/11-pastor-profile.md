# Brief — Pastor Profile (+ wellbeing + 360)

1. **Positioning.** An honest read of a pastor's whole life and ministry across 3 pillars / 14 domains, with a restrained wellbeing insert and an optional 360. Worked through with a coach.
2. **Archetype.** Composite multi-part.
3. **Signature visual.** The **three-pillar architecture** — Character / Competence / Contribution drawn as three literal columns, each carrying its domains as stacked capitals; the composite is the entablature they hold up. No other report is architectural.
4. **Cover.** Three stone-toned columns in deep-navy line, gold light between them.
5. **Rhythm.** Composite. Pillars hero → per-pillar domain detail → **restrained wellbeing insert** (Phase 4 rules: no decoration, severity band, support-first) → optional **360 blind-spot dumbbell** → prayer/Scripture.
6. **Accent / texture.** Deep navy `#122A44` / stone-column line motif.
7. **Bespoke.** Clinical wellbeing insert (restrained); 360 self-vs-observer dumbbell; prayer; domain-clusters. Wellbeing is quarantined visually and by rule.
8. **Target pages.** Long — 9–13 pages.
9. **Must NOT look like.** Church Planter. Kept apart per Planter brief. The wellbeing insert must not look like the celebratory sections around it — see Phase 4.
