# Brief — Fivefold Calling

1. **Positioning.** The believer's primary and secondary Ephesians-4 calling (APEST), read once to find their lane in the body.
2. **Archetype.** Narrative-led profile.
3. **Signature visual.** The **APEST five-lane calling spectrum** — five horizontal lanes (Apostle/Prophet/Evangelist/Shepherd/Teacher) with the person's score as a filled run in each and the **primary lane lit gold**, secondary outlined. A single-image verdict, not a chart wall.
4. **Cover.** Five thin vertical gold rules of ascending height (the five callings), the tallest lit; teal field.
5. **Rhythm.** Spare and confident. One dominant spectrum, then two focused narrative spreads (primary, secondary) with "where it goes wrong" shadow copy. Airy.
6. **Accent / texture.** Teal `#2E7D8A` / fine horizontal rule field.
7. **Bespoke.** Scripture spine (Ephesians 4) down section openers; shadow/watch copy per calling. (No character card, no radar.)
8. **Target pages.** Medium — 5–7 pages.
9. **Must NOT look like.** Spiritual Gifts. Kept apart by density (spare vs encyclopedic), accent (teal vs gold), and signature (5-lane spectrum vs illuminated ladder).
