# Brief — CHC Spiritual Gifts

1. **Positioning.** A believer's ranked map of all 25 spiritual gifts, read once and returned to when discerning where to serve. Read by lay members and ministry leaders placing them.
2. **Archetype.** Formational & scriptural.
3. **Signature visual.** An **illuminated top-3 gift triptych** — the three strongest gifts as three tall "illuminated capital" panels (serif drop-initial, gift name, score anchored to /max, its Scripture) — sitting above a full **gift ladder**: all 25 ranked as a calibrated column with a scale line and the top-3 lit. No other report uses illuminated capitals or a 25-deep ladder.
4. **Cover.** Deep-navy field, a single gold illuminated initial of the top gift emerging from parchment grain; title in Fraunces.
5. **Rhythm.** Airy up top (triptych gets a near-full page), then denser as the 25-list and per-gift study accrue. Section openers carry a Scripture line.
6. **Accent / texture.** Gold `#C4923E` / parchment grain.
7. **Bespoke.** Scripture callout + ministry-role card per gift; growth step per gift. (No character card, no gap, no clusters.)
8. **Target pages.** Long — 10–14 print pages (25 gifts earn it; the catalog is the point).
9. **Must NOT look like.** Fivefold Calling (also scriptural, also ranked). Kept apart: Gifts is a *deep 25-item catalog* with illuminated capitals + gold/parchment; Fivefold is a *5-lane spectrum*, teal, spare. Gifts is dense and encyclopedic; Fivefold is a single clean verdict.
