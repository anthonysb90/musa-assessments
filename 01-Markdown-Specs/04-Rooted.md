## What it measures

Rooted is a mirror, not a test. Over 40 statements you'll reflect on eight biblical markers of spiritual maturity, and see where you're deeply planted and where your roots still need to go down.

It looks at:

- 8 biblical markers of maturity
- From Abiding in Christ to Perseverance in Trials
- Per-marker bands, no single grade

**Domains / scales (8):** Abiding in Christ, Word & Prayer, Fruit of the Spirit, Community & Accountability, Serving & Gifts, Witness & Mission, Stewardship, Perseverance in Trials.

## The questions

All 40 items, in order, grouped by domain. This is the full item bank as it runs live.

**Abiding in Christ**  (5 items)

1. I stay spiritually connected to Christ throughout my normal day, not just in church settings.
2. When I feel distant from God, I notice it and take steps to reconnect.
3. My relationship with Jesus feels alive, not routine or empty.
4. I depend on Christ for my daily strength, not just my own effort.
5. I regularly sense God's presence in ordinary moments, not only in worship services.

**Word & Prayer**  (5 items)

6. I read Scripture on a regular basis, not only when I'm preparing to teach or preach.
7. My prayer life includes listening to God, not only asking Him for things.
8. Scripture regularly shapes decisions I make, not just my beliefs.
9. I have a consistent time set aside for prayer and the Word.
10. When life gets busy, prayer and Scripture are among the first things I protect, not the first things I drop.

**Fruit of the Spirit**  (5 items)

11. People who know me well would say my character has grown over the last year.
12. I respond to conflict with patience more often than I used to.
13. I can name specific ways I have grown in love, joy, or peace recently.
14. Self-control shows up in my daily habits, not just my public behavior.
15. I am quicker to show kindness and gentleness than I was a year ago.

**Community & Accountability**  (5 items)

16. I have people in my life who know the real me, not just my public role.
17. Someone has permission to ask me hard questions and expect an honest answer.
18. I am currently in real community, not just attending church services.
19. I have confessed a struggle to someone else in the last year.
20. I would notice, and be bothered, if I drifted from accountability.

**Serving & Gifts**  (5 items)

21. I am actively using my gifts to serve others, not just aware of what they are.
22. Serving feels like an offering to God, not an obligation I'm fulfilling.
23. I look for ways to serve outside of my formal ministry role.
24. I have said yes to serving even when it wasn't convenient.
25. I recognize the gifts in others and encourage them to use their gifts.

**Witness & Mission**  (5 items)

26. I have shared my faith with someone outside the church in the last year.
27. I pray regularly for specific people who don't know Christ.
28. My life outside of church reflects the same faith I profess inside it.
29. I look for natural opportunities to talk about what God is doing in my life.
30. I care personally about people coming to know Christ, not just about church growth numbers.

**Stewardship**  (5 items)

31. I give financially in a way that reflects trust in God, not just leftover margin.
32. I manage my time in a way that honors God's priorities, not just my own.
33. I am a faithful steward of what God has given me, even when no one is watching.
34. I make financial decisions with eternity in mind, not just comfort.
35. I have said no to something I wanted so I could be faithful with what I already have.

**Perseverance in Trials**  (5 items)

36. My faith has grown stronger through hard seasons, not just survived them.
37. I keep trusting God even when I don't understand what He's doing.
38. I have walked through a real trial in the last few years and can point to what it taught me.
39. I don't abandon spiritual practices just because life gets difficult.
40. I have hope that endures even when circumstances don't improve quickly.


## How it is scored

**Type:** Domain averages with bands.

1. Every scored answer uses the 1–5 response scale above.
2. Reverse-scored items (marked in the question list) are flipped before scoring: `adjusted = 1 + 5 − raw`.
3. Items are grouped by their **domain**. This assessment has 8 domains: Abiding in Christ, Word & Prayer, Fruit of the Spirit, Community & Accountability, Serving & Gifts, Witness & Mission, Stewardship, Perseverance in Trials.
4. Each domain score is the **average** of its item values (rounded to two decimals).
5. Each domain average is placed in a band:

| Band | Cutoff (average) |
|---|---|
| Deeply Rooted | ≥ 4 |
| Growing | ≥ 2.75 |
| Needs Watering | ≥ 0 |

6. Domains are sorted highest average to lowest. The strongest two are affirmed; the lowest two are the growth focus.

### Worked example

Suppose the 'Abiding in Christ' domain has 5 items and the taker answers 5, 4, 4, 5, 4 (no reverse items):
average = (5+4+4+5+4) / 5 = 4.40 → **Deeply Rooted** band. Repeated for every domain, then sorted.

## The report

The report is a single, colorful, print-ready page:
- A header band with the taker's name and date.
- **Your roots, marker by marker** — every domain listed with its average, its band color, and a short bar.
- **Where you're deeply rooted** — the top two domains, affirmed.
- **Where to grow** — the lowest two domains, each with a next step.
- A radar chart plots every domain so the whole shape is visible at a glance.
- Helper note: _Every grower has roots still going down somewhere. These are simply where the next season of growth is waiting, not a verdict on your walk._

Each domain carries a Scripture anchor and/or a concrete next step, shown for growth areas:

- **Abiding in Christ** _(Ref: John 15:4-5)_: Set one fixed anchor point in your day, morning or evening, to consciously reconnect with Christ before anything else pulls at you.
- **Word & Prayer** _(Ref: Joshua 1:8 · Psalm 1:2)_: Pick a short book of the Bible and read a few verses at the same time each day this week, ending with two minutes of silent listening.
- **Fruit of the Spirit** _(Ref: Galatians 5:22-23)_: Name the one fruit you most want to grow, then ask someone close to you to point out honestly where they see it and where they don't.
- **Community & Accountability** _(Ref: Proverbs 27:17 · Hebrews 10:24-25)_: Invite one trusted person into a standing monthly check-in where they have permission to ask you hard questions.
- **Serving & Gifts** _(Ref: 1 Peter 4:10)_: Say yes to one concrete way to serve this month that uses a gift you already know you have.
- **Witness & Mission** _(Ref: Matthew 5:16 · 1 Peter 3:15)_: Write down two people who don't yet know Christ, pray for them by name daily, and look for one natural opening to share.
- **Stewardship** _(Ref: Luke 16:10-11 · 2 Corinthians 9:7)_: Review where your time and money actually went last month and choose one adjustment that reflects trust in God over comfort.
- **Perseverance in Trials** _(Ref: James 1:2-4 · Romans 5:3-5)_: Name one hard season God carried you through and write down what it taught you, so the next trial meets a settled memory.

Colors: teal = strong band, gold = middle band, slate = lowest band.

Every report carries the Mission USA credit line: _"Assessment by Mission USA of the Congregational Holiness Church. Get yours at assessments.chchurch.com."_ Reports can be printed or saved as PDF, and are saved to the taker's profile.
