## How to take it

1. Go to assessments.chchurch.com and open **Church Planter — Assessor Rating** (or use a direct/embed link).
2. This instrument is delivered by **invite only** (text or email) as part of the parent assessment. It is not listed publicly.
3. Answer all 13 questions on the 1–5 scale below, 15 per page.
5. Submit. Your report is generated immediately and shown on screen and emailed to you as a link.

## The questions

All 13 items, in order, grouped by domain. This is the full item bank as it runs live.

**Visioning Capacity**  (1 items)

1. Has started things that did not exist, and made others want to build them.

**Intrinsically Motivated**  (1 items)

2. Pursues goals over the long haul with no one supervising.

**Creates Ownership of Ministry**  (1 items)

3. Develops others who then lead on their own.

**Reaches the Unchurched**  (1 items)

4. Builds real friendships with people far from God.

**Spousal Cooperation**  (1 items)

5. Is united with their spouse on the call to plant.

**Effectively Builds Relationships**  (1 items)

6. Builds trust and relationships across different kinds of people.

**Committed to Church Growth**  (1 items)

7. Has changed methods in order to reach more people.

**Responsive to the Community**  (1 items)

8. Shapes ministry around a community's real, specific needs.

**Utilizes the Giftedness of Others**  (1 items)

9. Places people in roles that fit their gifts.

**Flexible and Adaptable**  (1 items)

10. Adapts when plans fall apart, without losing the mission.

**Builds Group Cohesiveness**  (1 items)

11. Forms groups that become genuinely close and lasting.

**Resilience**  (1 items)

12. Recovers from real failure and keeps going.

**Exercises Faith**  (1 items)

13. Takes real steps of faith that depend on God coming through.


## How it is scored

**Type:** Co-rater input. This instrument is **not taken standalone** and is **not published** on the public site. It feeds the **Church Planter** report.

1. Sent by invite (text or email) to the trained assessor.
2. Answers use the 1–5 scale across 13 areas: Visioning Capacity, Intrinsically Motivated, Creates Ownership of Ministry, Reaches the Unchurched, Spousal Cooperation, Effectively Builds Relationships, Committed to Church Growth, Responsive to the Community, Utilizes the Giftedness of Others, Flexible and Adaptable, Builds Group Cohesiveness, Resilience, Exercises Faith.
3. Each area score is the **average** of its items.
4. These averages are placed **alongside the candidate/leader's own scores** in the parent report, so self-view and outside-view sit side by side and blind spots become visible.

### Worked example

If the leader rates their own 'Conflict Navigation' 4.5 but two peers average 3.0, the 1.5 gap is the blind spot the report surfaces.

## The report

There is no separate report for this instrument. Its scores appear inside the **Church Planter** report as a second (and third) column next to the primary taker's self-assessment. The gap between self-view and observer-view is the insight.

Every report carries the Mission USA credit line: _"Assessment by Mission USA of the Congregational Holiness Church. Get yours at assessments.chchurch.com."_ Reports can be printed or saved as PDF, and are saved to the taker's profile.
