## What it measures

A clear, honest look at your readiness to plant a church, across thirteen characteristics that consistently mark effective planters, through your answers, your spouse's perspective, and a trained assessor.

It looks at:

- 13 planter characteristics, primary five weighted
- Candidate, spouse, and assessor together
- A validity layer that rewards honesty

**Domains / scales (15):** Visioning Capacity, Intrinsically Motivated, Creates Ownership of Ministry, Reaches the Unchurched, Spousal Cooperation, Effectively Builds Relationships, Committed to Church Growth, Responsive to the Community, Utilizes the Giftedness of Others, Flexible and Adaptable, Builds Group Cohesiveness, Resilience, Exercises Faith, Candor, Attention.

## How to take it

1. Go to assessments.chchurch.com and open **Church Planter** (or use a direct/embed link).
2. Enter your name and contact details (used to save your report to your profile and to email you the link).
3. Answer all 73 questions on the 1–5 scale below, 15 per page.
5. Submit. Your report is generated immediately and shown on screen and emailed to you as a link.

## How it is scored

**Type:** Weighted composite + readiness tier + validity layer.

1. Answers use the 1–5 scale. Trait items are scored; **candor and attention-check items are not scored** (they feed the validity layer).
2. Items grouped by characteristic (13 total). Each characteristic score is the **average** of its items.
3. Five **primary** characteristics are weighted **2×**; the other eight are weighted **1×**:
   - Primary: Visioning Capacity, Intrinsically Motivated, Creates Ownership of Ministry, Reaches the Unchurched, Spousal Cooperation.
4. Composite = weighted average of all characteristics.
5. If the candidate is single, the 'Spousal Cooperation' characteristic is dropped from scoring.
6. **Readiness tier** (developmental, never a pass/fail verdict):
   - **Ready to Plant** — composite ≥ 4.0, weakest primary ≥ 3.75, no primary below 3.0.
   - **Develop First** — composite ≥ 3.25 with at most one primary below 3.0.
   - **Better Suited Elsewhere For Now** — otherwise.
7. **Validity layer** (assessor-only, never shown to the candidate): counts high self-favorable 'candor' answers, an attention check ('select Disagree'), and an infrequency check ('I have succeeded at everything I set out to do').

### Worked example

If the five primaries average 4.2 and secondaries 3.6, composite = (4.2×2×5 + 3.6×1×8)/(2×5 + 1×8) ≈ 3.93. With one primary at 2.9, tier = 'Develop First'.

## The report

The candidate report shows a **13-spoke radar**, the strongest characteristics and the ones to watch, and a **readiness picture framed as development, never a verdict**. Primary characteristics are marked. The optional spouse and assessor inputs appear side by side when collected. The validity flags are visible only to the assessor. For this assessment, Mission USA is always the assessor.

Every report carries the Mission USA credit line: _"Assessment by Mission USA of the Congregational Holiness Church. Get yours at assessments.chchurch.com."_ Reports can be printed or saved as PDF, and are saved to the taker's profile.
