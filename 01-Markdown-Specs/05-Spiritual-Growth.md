## What it measures

A prayerful look at six spiritual disciplines every follower of Christ grows in. Over 60 short statements you'll reflect on your own walk, then see it drawn as a Discipleship Wheel, so you can tell where your faith is strong and where the next season of growth is waiting. Based on LifeWay's Spiritual Growth Assessment.

It looks at:

- 6 disciplines: Abide, the Word, Prayer, Fellowship, Witness, and Ministry
- Your own honest self-reflection, before God
- A Discipleship Wheel that shows the whole picture at a glance

**Domains / scales (6):** Abide in Christ, Live in the Word, Pray in Faith, Fellowship with Believers, Witness to the World, Minister to Others.

## How it is scored

**Type:** Domain averages with bands.

1. Every scored answer uses the 1–5 response scale above.
2. Reverse-scored items (marked in the question list) are flipped before scoring: `adjusted = 1 + 5 − raw`.
3. Items are grouped by their **domain**. This assessment has 6 domains: Abide in Christ, Live in the Word, Pray in Faith, Fellowship with Believers, Witness to the World, Minister to Others.
4. Each domain score is the **average** of its item values (rounded to two decimals).
5. Each domain average is placed in a band:

| Band | Cutoff (average) |
|---|---|
| Strength | ≥ 4 |
| Steady | ≥ 2.75 |
| Needs Attention | ≥ 0 |

6. Domains are sorted highest average to lowest. The strongest two are affirmed; the lowest two are the growth focus.

### Worked example

Suppose the 'Abide in Christ' domain has 5 items and the taker answers 5, 4, 4, 5, 4 (no reverse items):
average = (5+4+4+5+4) / 5 = 4.40 → **Strength** band. Repeated for every domain, then sorted.

## The report

The report is a single, colorful, print-ready page:
- A header band with the taker's name and date.
- **Your six disciplines** — every domain listed with its average, its band color, and a short bar.
- **Where you're strongest** — the top two domains, affirmed.
- **Where to grow next** — the lowest two domains, each with a next step.
- A **Discipleship Wheel** radar chart plots all six disciplines at once.
- Helper note: _This is a mirror for one moment in your journey, not a grade on your walk with God. Every disciple has areas still filling in. These are simply where the next bit of growth is waiting._

Each domain carries a Scripture anchor and/or a concrete next step, shown for growth areas:

- **Abide in Christ** _(Ref: John 15:4-5)_: Set one fixed time this week to be with Christ with no agenda, not sermon prep, not a task, just abiding. Guard it like a meeting.
- **Live in the Word** _(Ref: Joshua 1:8 · 2 Timothy 3:16-17)_: Pick one short book of the Bible and read a few verses at the same time each day, ending with one question: what does this ask of me today?
- **Pray in Faith** _(Ref: Philippians 4:6-7 · James 5:16)_: Add two minutes of listening to the end of your prayers this week. Say less, and give God room to speak.
- **Fellowship with Believers** _(Ref: Hebrews 10:24-25 · Proverbs 27:17)_: Invite one trusted person into a standing check-in where they have permission to ask you hard questions and you actually answer.
- **Witness to the World** _(Ref: Matthew 28:19-20 · 1 Peter 3:15)_: Write down two people who don't yet know Christ, pray for them by name daily, and look for one natural opening to share this month.
- **Minister to Others** _(Ref: 1 Peter 4:10 · Matthew 25:40)_: Say yes to one concrete way to serve this month that uses a gift you already have, and do it without expecting anything back.

Colors: teal = strong band, gold = middle band, slate = lowest band.

Every report carries the Mission USA credit line: _"Assessment by Mission USA of the Congregational Holiness Church. Get yours at assessments.chchurch.com."_ Reports can be printed or saved as PDF, and are saved to the taker's profile.
