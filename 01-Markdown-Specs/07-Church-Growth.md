## What it measures

Not where you hope it's headed, where the evidence says it's headed. 50 statements, mixed so no stage is identifiable while you answer, give an honest read on your church's current stage, from decline to multiplication.

It looks at:

- 5 stages: Decline, Plateaued, Adding, Reproducing, Multiplying
- Each stage scored independently
- A transition flag when you're between stages

**Levels / stages (5):** Level 3 — A Church Adding, Level 1 — A Church in Decline, Level 5 — A Church Multiplying, Level 2 — A Church Plateaued, Level 4 — A Church Reproducing Campuses.

## How it is scored

**Type:** Highest-column level match (5 stages).

1. Answers use the 0–3 scale (Not at all → Consistently).
2. Every item belongs to one of five **levels** (its `domain` is the level number 1–5). Items are deliberately mixed so no stage is identifiable while answering.
3. Each level's score is the **sum** of its items.
4. The **winning level** is the highest-scoring column (ties break toward the more mature/higher level).
5. A **transition flag** is set when an adjacent level is within 4 points of the winner — meaning the church straddles two stages.

| 2 | A Church Plateaued | Please Stay |
| 3 | A Church Adding | Please Come |
| 4 | A Church Reproducing Campuses | Please Come |
| 5 | A Church Multiplying | Please GO |

### Worked example

If Level 3 (Adding) = 24 and Level 4 (Reproducing) = 18, winner = Adding; since they differ by 6 (> 4), no transition flag.

## The report

The report names the **current stage and its message** (Please Stay / Please Come / Please GO), shows **all five stages side by side**, describes the stage, and points to what it takes to reach the next one. If a transition is detected, it says the church is between two stages.

Every report carries the Mission USA credit line: _"Assessment by Mission USA of the Congregational Holiness Church. Get yours at assessments.chchurch.com."_ Reports can be printed or saved as PDF, and are saved to the taker's profile.
