## What it measures

Pastoring asks everything of a person. Pastor Profile looks at all of it, honestly and without judgment, across three pillars and fourteen domains, plus a private, confidential read on how you're really doing.

It looks at:

- 14 domains across Character, Competence, and Contribution
- A three-pillar balance view
- A confidential wellbeing check, private to you

**Domains / scales (15):** Calling & Conviction, Character & Integrity, Spiritual Vitality, Emotional Health, Rest, Rhythm & Health, Marriage & Family, Preaching & Teaching, Pastoral Care & Shepherding, Leadership & Vision, Conflict Resolution, Communication & Relationships, Disciple-Making & Multiplication, Mentoring & Accountability, Mission & Evangelism, Wellbeing.

## How to take it

1. Go to assessments.chchurch.com and open **Pastor Profile** (or use a direct/embed link).
2. Enter your name and contact details (used to save your report to your profile and to email you the link).
3. Answer all 61 questions on the 1–5 scale below, 20 per page.
4. Because this assessment is sensitive, you must be **signed in**. Results are gated to your account.
5. Submit. Your report is generated immediately and shown on screen and emailed to you as a link.

## How it is scored

**Type:** Pillar / domain averages, plus a private wellbeing module.

1. Answers use the 1–5 scale.
2. Items grouped into 14 **domains**; each domain score is its **average**, banded Strength / Steady / Needs Attention (≥4.0 / ≥2.75 / below).
3. Domains roll up into three **pillars**: Character & Inner Life, Competence, Contribution & Support. Each pillar score is the average of its domains.
4. A separate **Wellbeing module** (0–3 per item, last two weeks) is **quarantined**: it is scored and stored apart, owner-only, and **never** enters the shared report, any export, or any email. Two items ('felt down/hopeless', 'wondered whether I can keep going') force a 'heavy load' band when elevated, which quietly alerts the Mission USA care contact.

### Worked example

If 'Spiritual Vitality' averages 4.3 → Strength. The Character & Inner Life pillar = average of its six domain averages.

## The report

The report gives a **three-pillar snapshot** across all 14 domains, names strengths and focus areas each with a next step, and shows the taker a **private read on their own wellbeing and rest**. Because it is sensitive, it requires sign-in. The confidential wellbeing section is visible only to the pastor and the care team — never to the local church.

Every report carries the Mission USA credit line: _"Assessment by Mission USA of the Congregational Holiness Church. Get yours at assessments.chchurch.com."_ Reports can be printed or saved as PDF, and are saved to the taker's profile.

## Data captured

- Contact block: name, email, phone, age band, ministry role, CHC yes/no, church (if any).
- Every individual answer (stored in `responses`), the scored result (`scored_json`), session timing, and source tag.
- Admins can view the full response set per person in the admin CRM. This is not disclosed on the public site.
- Confidential module answers (wellbeing / safety) are stored **separately**, owner-and-care-team only, and never appear in shared reports, exports, or emails.
