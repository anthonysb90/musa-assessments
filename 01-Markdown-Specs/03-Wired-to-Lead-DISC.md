## What it measures

A simple lens on how you naturally move through decisions, people, and pressure. Over 28 statements, Wired to Lead shows your DISC blend, paired with a biblical leader who shares it.

It looks at:

- Four dimensions: Drive, Influence, Steadiness, Conscientiousness
- One of 12 blends, each with a biblical parallel
- Your full wiring, not a flattened label

**Domains / scales (4):** D, I, S, C.

## How to take it

1. Go to assessments.chchurch.com and open **Wired to Lead** (or use a direct/embed link).
2. Enter your name and contact details (used to save your report to your profile and to email you the link).
3. Answer all 28 questions on the 1–5 scale below, 14 per page.
5. Submit. Your report is generated immediately and shown on screen and emailed to you as a link.

## The questions

All 28 items, in order, grouped by domain. This is the full item bank as it runs live.

**D**  (7 items)

1. I like being in charge and making the call.
2. I make decisions quickly and move forward without dwelling.
3. I welcome a challenge, especially one people doubt can be done.
4. I get straight to the point instead of talking around an issue.
5. I would rather lead an initiative than wait to be assigned one.
6. I push hard to reach a goal, even when it's uncomfortable for others.
7. I am comfortable taking a calculated risk to get results.

**I**  (7 items)

8. I build relationships easily and enjoy meeting new people.
9. I bring energy to a room and lift the mood of a meeting.
10. I can get people excited about a new idea or direction.
11. I would rather talk something through with others than work it out alone.
12. I stay hopeful and look for the upside, even in a hard season.
13. I enjoy encouraging people publicly, not just privately.
14. I use stories and illustrations naturally when I communicate.

**S**  (7 items)

15. I stay calm when things get stressful or chaotic.
16. I am loyal and stick with people and commitments for the long haul.
17. People know they can depend on me to follow through.
18. I would rather preserve peace than start a conflict, even when I probably should speak up.
19. I prefer steady, predictable routines over constant change.
20. I listen patiently before I respond, even under pressure.
21. I am the person people come to when they need someone calm in the room.

**C**  (7 items)

22. I want to understand all the facts before I make a decision.
23. I catch details and inconsistencies that other people miss.
24. I prefer doing something right over doing it fast.
25. I follow a clear process rather than winging it.
26. I hold myself and others to a high standard of accuracy.
27. I would rather have a well-researched plan than a bold guess.
28. I take time to think things through before I speak.


## How it is scored

**Type:** DISC blend.

1. Answers use the 1–5 scale (Not me at all → Completely me).
2. Items are grouped into four dimensions: **D**rive, **I**nfluence, **S**teadiness, **C**onscientiousness (7 items each).
3. Each dimension score is the **sum** of its items.
4. Dimensions ranked by score; ties break by priority D > I > S > C.
5. The top two form the **blend** (e.g., D + C = 'DC'). The blend maps to one of 12 biblical-leader profiles.

### Worked example

If D=30, C=28, I=20, S=18 → primary D, secondary C → blend 'DC' → Nehemiah, the Builder.

## The report

The report shows the **blend and its biblical parallel** (e.g., DC → Nehemiah, 'The Builder'), all four dimensions on a bar/radar, and for the blend: strengths, watch-outs, where the person is best used, and one growth challenge.

Every report carries the Mission USA credit line: _"Assessment by Mission USA of the Congregational Holiness Church. Get yours at assessments.chchurch.com."_ Reports can be printed or saved as PDF, and are saved to the taker's profile.
